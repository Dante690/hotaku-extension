# Privacy Policy — Hotaku Esports Odds Tracker

_Last updated: 2026-06-03_

Hotaku Esports Odds Tracker (the "Extension") is designed to respect your
privacy. This policy explains what the Extension does and does not do with data.

## Summary

The Extension **does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data.**
It has no analytics, no tracking, no accounts, and no advertising.

## What the Extension accesses

- **The active tab, only when you click the toolbar icon.** When you click the
  icon on a supported site (HLTV, Liquipedia, Twitch, op.gg), the Extension reads
  the page to identify the esports game and team names of the match shown. This
  happens locally, in your browser, and only on your explicit click.

## What is sent off your device

- **Only a game identifier**, to Hotaku's public API
  (`https://api.hotaku.fun/markets`), in order to fetch the public market odds for
  that game (for example, `?game=cs2`). No team names, URLs, page contents,
  identifiers, or personal information are transmitted.

## What is stored

- **Nothing.** The Extension does not use browser storage, cookies, or any
  persistent storage of your activity.

## Permissions

- `activeTab`: read the current page **only on your click**, to detect the match.
- Host access to `https://api.hotaku.fun`: fetch public, unauthenticated odds.

## Third parties

The Extension communicates only with Hotaku's public API to retrieve odds. It
does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children

The Extension is not directed at children and collects no data from anyone.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will be revised.
